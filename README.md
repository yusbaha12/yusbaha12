#### 1. KiddyDiary (Digital Diary App)
A beautifully designed application focused on tracking child developmental milestones. Features an intuitive UX and custom video bumpers, built to provide parents with a seamless tracking experience.

#### 2. Multi-Branch Clinic ERP & POS
Architected a comprehensive web-based Point of Sale and management system designed to scale across 10+ branches. Includes mobile application support, patient growth projections, and staff management modules.

Developed a warehouse management and Point of Sale (POS) system driven by unique QR-code integration for individual items. Implemented comprehensive quality assurance (QA) and quality control (QC) modules, managed technical workflows for the Engineering department, and integrated a robust corporate Asset Management System.
